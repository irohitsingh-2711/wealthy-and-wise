Coast FIRE Calculator

Find the point where you can stop investing entirely and let compounding carry you to retirement.

Coast FIRE is the point where your existing portfolio, left untouched, will grow to your FIRE number by retirement — without any further contributions. After this, you only need to cover living expenses.
